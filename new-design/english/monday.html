Creamy Pasta Primavera

Fresh spring vegetables with al dente pasta in a light cream sauce. Vegetarian friendly and perfect to start the week. This dish combines seasonal green vegetables like asparagus, peas, and zucchini with a delicate parmesan cream sauce that coats each pasta strand perfectly. Topped with fresh herbs and a squeeze of lemon, this dish is both satisfying and refreshing.
