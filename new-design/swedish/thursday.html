Classic Chicken Parmesan

Crispy breaded chicken cutlets topped with marinara sauce and melted mozzarella, served with spaghetti. Our chicken is tenderized, lightly breaded with Italian seasoned breadcrumbs, and pan-fried until golden. It's then baked with our slow-simmered tomato sauce and a generous layer of mozzarella and parmesan cheeses until bubbly. Served alongside perfectly cooked al dente spaghetti with extra marinara sauce.
